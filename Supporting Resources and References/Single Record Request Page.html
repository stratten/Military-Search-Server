Single Record Request

Use this page to request a Certificate verifying Active Duty Status for an individual on a specified date.

**SSN is NOT required. Unless you are a financial institution or otherwise required to collect SSNs, you should not require individuals to provide you with their SSN for the sole purpose of conducting a query on this website. Searches can be conducted using Last Name and Date of Birth alone. Providing additional details such as First Name, Middle Name, or SSN, if already known, will increase the accuracy of the match. If you do provide SSN, Date of Birth is not required, however including it can further improve the accuracy of the match.

Tips & Notes

  • Without a Social Security Number, DMDC cannot authoritatively assert that this is the same individual that your query refers to. Name and date of birth alone do not uniquely identify an individual.
  • Check your data entry before submitting it.
  • Response may take up to 15 seconds after clicking "Submit".
